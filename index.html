01 — About

A studio built on first principles, not industry templates.

We're a small, senior team of strategists, designers, and operators. No juniors staffed on your work, no recycled playbooks, no scope creep. Just rigorous thinking and uncompromising craft.

For nearly a decade, Meridian has helped founders, executives, and boards navigate the moments that define companies — a category shift, a fundraise, a relaunch, a scale-up that's hitting its ceiling.

We believe strategy without execution is theatre, and execution without strategy is gambling. So we do both. We embed with your team, ask the uncomfortable questions, and ship work that holds up under scrutiny.

Every engagement is led by a partner. Every recommendation is defensible. Every artifact is crafted. That's the standard.
